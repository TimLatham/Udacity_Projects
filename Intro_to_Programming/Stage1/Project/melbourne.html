Visiting the Melbourne Area

Why Melbourne?

My wife and I are planning a visit to the Melbourne area for our 40th birthdays. We visited Sydney on our honeymoon, and were highly recommended to return and visit Melbourne.

The Yarra Valley

We love wine touring as a great way to get out of town and see a bit of the world while enjoying tasty grape juice. The Yarra Valley is located only an hour or so from Melbourne, and has a great reputation for both wine and scenery.

The Great Ocean Road

The Great Ocean Road is a beautiful coastal road that features varying terrain including the famous Twelve Apostles, pictured to the left.

Melbourne food scene description

The Melbourne food scene is considered by many to be the trendiest in Australia. My wife is a hobbyist chef with some culinary training so of course we had to visit Melbourne, if only for that reason alone!
